Recipe for Delectable Wings

Description

A party favorite! Crispy wings with just the right amount of sauce, cooked to perfection! Speaking of sauce, it's bursting with flavor and with spicy sweetness. These wings are simple to cook but complex in palate. Photo by Chad Montano.

Ingredients

  • 4 Whole Chicken Wings
  • 1 Tbspoon of Lemon Juice
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • 2 Tbspoons of Pepper
  • 2 Tbspoons of Honey Glaze

Steps

  1. Mix the Honey Glaze with Lemon Juice and Pepper.
  2. Marinate the Wings in the Mixture.
  3. Air Fry the Wings with Crushed Garlic on Top.
  4. Serve.
