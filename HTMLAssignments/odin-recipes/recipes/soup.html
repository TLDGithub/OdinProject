Recipe for Tomato Soup

Description

A soup to warm the soul. Easy to make and fills you right up. This hearty soup is perfect for a cold night. Photo by Nathan Dumlao.

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Tomato
  • 2 Sprigs of Cilantro
  • 2 Slices of Ginger
  • 1 Tbspoon of Pepper
  • 1 Tbspoon of Salt
  • 1 Tbspoon of Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. Dice the Tomato.
  2. Mix the Diced Tomatoes with Salt, Pepper, Chicken Stock, and Water.
  3. Heat to a Boil.
  4. Add Ginger and Cilantro.
  5. Serve.
