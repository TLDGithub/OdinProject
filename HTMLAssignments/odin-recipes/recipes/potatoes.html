Recipe for Pan Seared Taters

Description

Another simple dish that fills the stomach. These potatos have a unique crispy skin but soft melting interior. Get them while they're hot. Photo by Abby Kale.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Potatoes
  • 1 Tbspoon Cajun Seasoning
  • 1 Sprig of Cilantro
  • 2 Tbspoons Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Tbspoons of Salt
  • 1 Glove of Garlic

Steps

  1. Dice and then Boil the Potatoes.
  2. Dry the Potatoes and Heat Pan.
  3. Add Vegetable Oil and then the Potatoes.
  4. Add Garlic.
  5. Sprinkle with Salt and Cajun Seasoning.
  6. Serve.
